Project Introduction

The Afdal Tawassul Team came together as part of THE Port 2020 online Humanitarian Hackathon, hosted virtually at CERN. We have developed an SMS-based automated feedback mechanism for gathering survey feedback from internally displaced people in Yemen. We have aimed to reach people currently receiving assistance from UNHCR and their partners including the World Food Programme (WFP), as well as those outside the system. Through enabling UN agenices to more effectively gather feedback, we hope that this tool will ultimately facilitate more data driven humanitarian programming and improve accountability to affected populations.
